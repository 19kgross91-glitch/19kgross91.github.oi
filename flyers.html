Philadelphia Flyers

This page is about my favorite team in the National Hockey League (NHL), the Philadelphia Flyers! They're often considered one of the most successful teams in the history of the sport, due to their high winning percentage. They won back-to-back Stanley Cups in 1974 and 1975 by being more physical than everybody else, which earned them the nickname The Broad Street Bullies. They've appeared in 6 more Cup finals since then, with the next victory hopefully soon.

Best players in franchise history:

  • Bobby Clarke
  • Bill Barber
  • Bernie Parent
  • Eric Lindros
  • Mark Howe
  • Claude Giroux
