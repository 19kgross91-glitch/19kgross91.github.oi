Welcome To My Website About My Favorite Sports And Teams!

As far back as I can remember, I've always loved sports! I live near Philadelphia, which has a reputation (good and bad) for having a passionate fanbase across the four major sports in America. There have been many highs and lows, but there is always something exciting going on. Some of the reasons why I love sports are the intensity, the high-stakes, and the way a team can bring a community together.
