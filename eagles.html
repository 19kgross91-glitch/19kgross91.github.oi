Philadelphia Eagles

This page is about my favorite team in the National Football League (NFL), the Philadelphia Eagles! They were founded in 1933 and won 3 championships during that time period. Beginning in 1967, the championship was renamed the Super Bowl. This is considered the modern era of the sport. There were many losing years, but the team has been very successful during my life. After a long drought, the Eagles have won 2 Super Bowls in the last 8 years:

  • Super Bowl 52 (LII)
  • Super Bowl 59(LIX)

The following are some notable players in franchise history:

  • Jason Kelce
  • Donovan McNabb
  • Brandon Graham
  • Mike Quick
  • Reggie White
  • Brian Dawkins
  • Chuck Bednarik
