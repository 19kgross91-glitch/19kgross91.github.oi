Philadelphia Phillies

This page is about my favorite team in Major League Baseball (MLB), the Philadelphia Phillies! The Phillies are a very old franchise, being founded in 1883. It's a good thing there were 2 professional baseball teams in the city until 1955, because the Phillies have been bad most of their existence. After 97 years, they finally won their first World Series in 1980, along with another one in 2008. Some of their best players include:

  • Ryan Howard
  • Chase Utley
  • Jimmy Rollins
  • Mike Schmidt
  • Steve Carlton
